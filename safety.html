We care about your safety (maybe)

"I think there's a 25% chance that things go really, really badly."

- Dario Amodei, CEO, Anthropic

We know that advanced AI is exceptionally dangerous! Experts and evidence clearly point to numerous threats - from bioterrorism and super scams, to mass unemployment and chatbot psychosis.

BUT reliable safeguards are expensive and time-consuming :/ and if we don't build superhuman AI quickly, someone else will do it first. We need to think about the shareholders.

Nevertheless, we are committed to your family's well-being (sort of), so we do what we can to protect you - as long as it doesn't mean slowing down or losing money.

*We have no obligations. Unlike other industries, from airlines to pharmaceuticals to sandwich shops, AI has no safety standards or regulations in the US. Zero. Nothing. Our carefully lobbied and generously funded lawmakers give us a free hand - carte blanche!
